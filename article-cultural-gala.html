Bridging European Culture: Scaling the Stage at the Romanian Operetta Gala

Event Date: January 2026

Location: Elbasan, Albania

At Cantech Informatics, we believe that strategic engineering is not just about silicon and software—it is about the people and the culture that connect our European continent. In late 2025, we were honored to serve as an official sponsor for the Romanian Operetta Gala, a landmark event that brought music, elegance, and European culture to the stage of Teatri "Skampa" in Elbasan, Albania.

"The event strengthened cultural ties between Albania and Romania, setting the stage for future artistic collaborations between the two countries."

A Fusion of Artistic Heritage

The gala officially opened Elbasan’s new artistic season with a unique performance. Artists from Bucharest’s National Theater of Operetta and Musical "Ion Dacian" were joined by popular Albanian singer Eli Fara, whose powerful interpretations combined Aromanian influences with Albanian spirit.

The program featured arias, duets, and well-known pieces that moved the audience, earning long applause and welcoming the start of a season full of art, culture, and emotion. Notable guests included the renowned conductor Zhani Ciko.

Why We Sponsor Cultural Ties

As a European engineering firm, our operations span across borders. Whether we are designing ultra-low power PMICs or implementing secure SoC architectures, our work relies on the same principles of collaboration and shared heritage that were on display at the Skampa Theater.

Sponsoring the Operetta Gala is our way of giving back to the community and reinforcing the "European Advantage"—the idea that our proximity, shared values, and historical ties make us more than just a services provider, but a true strategic partner.
